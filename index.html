Our Services

Water & Wastewater Treatment (Design–Build–Operate)

We design and build treatment systems tailored to raw-water characteristics and end-use requirements and can operate/maintain them under SLAs. Solutions range from compact community plants to large state facilities.

Scope:

  • Water quality assessment and process design
  • Package plants (10-5,000 m³/day capacity)
  • Conventional treatment (coagulation, sedimentation, filtration)
  • Advanced treatment (membrane filtration, UV disinfection)
  • Industrial wastewater treatment and reuse

Methods/Technology:

  • PLC/SCADA automation and remote monitoring
  • Energy-efficient variable frequency drives
  • Advanced process control algorithms
  • IoT sensors for real-time optimization
  • Mobile monitoring and alert systems

Outcomes:

95%+ production capacity achievement
30-50% reduction in chemical consumption
98%+ uptime with automated controls
WHO/NSDWQ compliance guaranteed

Hydro-Technical Development (Dams, Storage & Reservoirs)

We provide planning, design, and construction for water storage and control infrastructure; dams, raw-water reservoirs, clear-water tanks, and elevated steel tanks for gravity distribution.

Scope:

  • Small to medium dams and weirs (height 5-20m)
  • Raw water intake and pump stations
  • Elevated steel tanks (50-2,000 m³)
  • Ground reservoirs and clear water storage
  • Spillways and hydraulic control structures

Methods/Technology:

  • 3D hydraulic modeling and flood analysis
  • Geotechnical investigation and soil testing
  • Structural design with seismic considerations
  • Cathodic protection for steel structures
  • Remote level monitoring and SCADA integration

Outcomes:

100% structural integrity compliance
25+ year design life with minimal maintenance
Zero safety incidents during construction
Optimized water storage and distribution

Pipeline Distribution & Reticulation

We design and construct transmission mains and distribution networks, including Ward metered areas (WMAs), bulk metering, valve chambers, and household connections.

Scope:

  • Transmission mains (200-1000mm diameter)
  • Distribution networks and reticulation
  • Ward metered areas (WMA) and bulk metering
  • House connections and service lines
  • Valve chambers and pressure reducing stations

Methods/Technology:

  • Hydraulic network modeling (EPANET/WaterGEMS)
  • Trenchless boring for road/railway crossings
  • Multiple pipe materials (uPVC/DI/HDPE/steel)
  • GPS/GIS asset mapping and documentation
  • Pressure testing and leak detection

Outcomes:

18+ KM of pipeline successfully delivered
NRW reduction below 20% in target areas
24/7 reliable water supply to communities
15,000+ households connected

Smart Metering & Revenue Protection

In partnership with technology providers, we deploy prepaid/postpaid smart meters and billing platforms to improve revenue collection and customer experience.

Scope:

  • Smart meter deployment (residential & commercial)
  • AMR/AMI system integration
  • Customer billing and payment platforms
  • Revenue assurance and tamper detection
  • Customer training and support services

Methods/Technology:

  • NB-IoT and LoRaWAN communication protocols
  • Cloud-based data management and analytics
  • Mobile and web-based customer applications
  • Automated billing and payment systems
  • Real-time leak detection and alerts

Outcomes:

25,000+ smart meters successfully deployed
40-60% improvement in revenue collection
Real-time tamper detection and alerts
Enhanced customer experience via mobile apps

Roads & Multi-Utility Corridors
