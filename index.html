Our Services

Comprehensive Engineering Solutions

End-to-end EPC services for utilities and civil infrastructure across Nigeria and West Africa

Water & Wastewater Treatment (Design–Build–Operate)

We design and build treatment systems tailored to raw-water characteristics and end-use requirements and can operate/maintain them under SLAs. Solutions range from compact community plants to large state facilities.

Key Capabilities:

  • Source assessment and treatability studies
  • Multi-stage filtration and advanced oxidation
  • PLC/SCADA integration and remote monitoring
  • Energy-efficient pumps and process controls
  • Comprehensive O&M and operator training

Outcomes & KPIs:

Stable production to target capacity
Lower chemical and energy use
Improved uptime and simplified operations

Hydro-Technical Development (Dams, Storage & Reservoirs)

We provide planning, design, and construction for water storage and control infrastructure; dams, raw-water reservoirs, clear-water tanks, and elevated steel tanks for gravity distribution.

Scope of Work:

  • Hydrology/hydraulic analysis and geotechnical investigations
  • Spillways, intake structures, and outlet works
  • Elevated steel tanks and ground reservoirs
  • Corrosion protection and instrumentation
  • Hydraulic modelling and GIS-supported siting

Pipeline Distribution & Reticulation

We design and construct transmission mains and distribution networks, including Ward metered areas (WMAs), bulk metering, valve chambers, and household connections.

Key Features:

  • Network modelling and pipe sizing
  • Trenchless crossings using thrust boring
  • Multiple pipe materials (uPVC/DI/HDPE/steel)
  • GPS/GIS asset mapping
  • Complete house connections and metering

Smart Metering & Revenue Protection

In partnership with technology providers, we deploy prepaid/postpaid smart meters and billing platforms to improve revenue collection and customer experience.

Track Record:

  • 25,000+ smart meters deployed across multiple states
  • AMR/AMI configuration and integration
  • Revenue assurance and tamper detection
  • Automated billing and reduced NRW
  • Customer education and utility staff training

Flood Risk Management & Urban Drainage

We mitigate flood risk with resilient drainage systems, early-warning concepts, and nature-based solutions that protect lives and infrastructure.

Solutions Include:

  • Catchment analysis and flood risk assessments
  • Drainage masterplans and detention basins
  • Green infrastructure and bioswales
  • GIS mapping and hydrological modelling
  • Water-level alerts and monitoring systems

Road Construction & Ancillary Civil Works

Our civil works team delivers new road construction and rehabilitation tied to utility projects, industrial estates, and public infrastructure.

Capabilities:

  • Topographic surveys and geometric design
  • Pavement and subgrade design
  • Earthworks and asphalt/concrete paving
  • Side drains and erosion control
  • QA/QC testing and compliance
